Approach



The project works by taking meeting input in the form of audio, video, or text.



If the input is audio or video, it is converted into text using speech-to-text. Then the transcript is processed to generate summary and extract action items.



For action items, the system tries to identify who is responsible and what needs to be done.



A chatbot is also added where users can ask questions based on the transcript. The system searches the transcript and gives answers with references.



The goal is to make meeting data easy to understand and reduce time spent reading long transcripts.
